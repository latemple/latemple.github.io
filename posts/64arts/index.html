KAILASA OM BHUSHAN ART AWARDS

KAILASA OM BHUSHAN AWARDS for 64 Vedic Arts is the highest award in KAILASA conferred to distinguished individuals for their excellence in contributions to traditional 64 Vedic Arts.

The award is given by Department of Religion and Arts, the Soverign State of SHRIKAILASA.

The nomination process is open to the public. Even self-nomination can be made. To nominate Click Here

  3. Nritya vidya: art of dancing.
